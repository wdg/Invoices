Customers

Keep a list of your customers so you don't have to enter their billing and shipping information over and over again. They will be automatically added to your database every time you create an invoice.

You can also enter records manually by filling in the fields and saving the form, the list will be updated instantly.

If you want to select a customer for your new invoice, just select it from the list and click the [Select] icon at the top in the action toolbar, the customer info will be populated in your invoice for you.
